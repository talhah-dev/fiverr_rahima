Process

How we work, start to finish

A clear path from first message to final files. Built to be fast, friendly, and predictable.

Step 1

Brief

Tell us your date, venue, and what you’re recording. Share runtimes and any constraints.

  • Date and location
  • Package or needs
Step 2

Plan

We confirm availability, refine the mic plan, and lock timings. Add-ons as needed.

  • Availability confirmation
  • Simple quote
Step 3

Capture

Deliver
